Democratic Primary

The places where Bernie did best were very liberal, not very affluent, and mostly minority; stereotypical college towns + rural western Massachusetts near Vermont. But he also did well in relatively Republican areas, especially less affluent; better correlated with the 2016 general election than 2012. This suggests he did better in places where the Democratic Party as an instution was weaker.
